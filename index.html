IMPERSIVE

Impersive, founded in 2014 by Guido Geminiani, specializes in VR video production, offering immersive experiences through 360 stereoscopic 3D videos with full-body motion. Their advanced technology is able to create engaging virtual environments for users to explore.

The making of a vr video:

Deciding the theme: This initial stage involves determining the concept or narrative for the VR video, setting the direction for the project.

Filming: Once the theme is established, the filming stage begins, where footage is captured using advanced 360-degree cameras. This step focuses on capturing scenes and interactions within the virtual environment.

Editing: After filming, the footage is edited with a stiching software.
